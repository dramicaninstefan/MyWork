Soybean Cake

Soybean cake is a protein-rich feed. It is moderately rich in energy.

It is intended for all categories of domestic animals, especially during the growth phase.

By introducing soybean cake into animal feed mixtures, up to 10% better production results (growth, egg number and weight, milk, and even reproductive properties) can be achieved compared to mixtures based on soybean meal. At the same time, the feed consumption per unit of product (conversion) is also reduced.

All this clearly indicates the economic justification of introducing soybean cake into the diets of domestic animals.

Raw Oil

Raw oil is obtained by extruding and pressing raw seeds of various plants. Depending on the type of raw material, its properties, color, and storage method vary.

Soybean oil is obtained by extruding and pressing soybean seeds.
Rapeseed oil is obtained by pressing rapeseed.
Sunflower oil is obtained by pressing sunflower seeds.

All oils are stored in tanks made of high-density polyethylene, and their shelf life is 6 months.
